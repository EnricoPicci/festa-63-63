# Where to Stay

For those who want to extend the magic (or aren't in any condition to drive after the toast), here are some options nearby:

- **Casa de Eli** | B&B | ~2 min | Budget-mid | *So close you'll hear the toasting*
- **Hotel Diana** | Hotel | ~5 min (Valdobbiadene centre) | 65-115 EUR | *Classic and reliable, like Prosecco*
- **Agriturismo Vigneto Vecio** | Agriturismo | ~10 min (Santo Stefano) | from ~63 EUR | *63 euros for 63 years: coincidence?*
- **Agriturismo Vedova** | Agriturismo | ~5-10 min | ~90-130 EUR | *9.3 on Booking: higher than our wine rating*
- **Locanda Sandi** | Inn | ~5-10 min (Villa Sandi estate) | 70-120 EUR | *Sleeping in a wine estate: consistency*
- **Agriturismo Due Carpini** | Agriturismo+Spa | ~10-12 min (Cartizze area) | ~120-200 EUR | *For those who want a spa after the Prosecco*
- **Boutique Hotel Municipio 1815** | Boutique Hotel | ~10-15 min (San Pietro di Barbozza) | from ~210 EUR | *Vineyard views all the way to Venice*
- **Hotel Villa Soligo** | Luxury Hotel | ~12-15 min (Farra di Soligo) | 150-300+ EUR | *For those who drink Prosecco from crystal flutes*

All distances from Agriturismo da Ottavio. Prices are approximate, check when booking.
