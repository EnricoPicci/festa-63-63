# Goal of the app
This app implements a website that describes a party that will happen in June.
The website must be mobile friendly.
## The background
The party will be held by 4 friends that were born in 1963 and that, this year, will turn 63.
Therefore the title of the party is "63-63".
This is the 4th party these friends hold. The first one was held when they turned 40, the second when they turned 50, the third when they turned 60 and now this one.
These 4 parties have been held alwasy in the same location, at "Agriturismo da Ottavio" in Via Campion, 1, 31049 Valdobbiadene TV.
## The guys
The four guys got to know each other in Padua, during secondary school, where all of them attended Liceo Tito Livio. Since those years that have remained closed friend, the best friends for each other.
- Giovanni: he is has a degree in Philosophy and is a journalist. He is nostalgic and very right wing in politics. He had a lot of girlfriends, a couple of daughters, but now is stable with a great girl that is a lawyer.
- Enrico: he is an engineer who now is deep into AI. 
- Stefano: he is also a degree in Philosophy and is a University Professor of Political Science. From a political perspective he is very left wing, the opposite of Giovanni.
- Gigio: he has a degree in Economics and is a Professor of Finance at University of Chicago
### Pictures
You can find some pictures of the 4 guys in the folder "pictures"

## The tone
The website must both evocative and funny.
Evocative when it comes to Bigolino, the Prosecco region, the surroundings.
Funny when it talks about the 4 guys.
The link between the 4 guys and the region is that all of them like wine.
